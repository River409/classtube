### Making an Account
To make an account on ClassTube you must have a valid montessori school e-mail. Of course ending in "XXXX@montessori-academico.es". Any account that violates this policy will get terminated or deleted from the account database. You cannot make a spam account, or use the same email on  a different account. On creating an account, you must put your real birthdate.
### Uploading Videos
On classtube, uploading videos is no problem, the problem starts when someone uploads an inappropriate video to the said platform. Another problem is spam videos. When an account has more than 10 videos uploaded. The uploader will get a warning that they're overloading the website. A user can copyright their video. So no other users take the video. Any account that violates this policy will get terminated or deleted from the account database or may recieve temporary suspension and an email warning them.
On upload the person who uploaded the video must have authorisation of the people in the video. If someone doesn't want to appear in the video, if so, the person must email the user "Admin" asking to take down the video.
### Reposting Videos
You must not repost videos on other websites if the people in the video have not authorised the use of their face on another platform.
